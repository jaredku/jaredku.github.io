01 — Overview

Why I Built This

The summer after my freshman year, I set out to build a 3-DOF robotic arm entirely from scratch — mechanical design, electronics, and software. The project was self-initiated and self-funded, with a budget cap of $150 to keep the build realistic and force intentional design decisions. The motivation came from a robotics class I took in high school using VEX, where I loved working through problems creatively with my team. I wanted to revisit that interest independently and see how far I could push it on my own.
