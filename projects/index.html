Wind Turbine

An engineering class project focused on designing and building a small-scale wind turbine optimized for power output.

The project covered blade geometry and aerodynamic profiling, rotor assembly, and testing under controlled airflow conditions. I applied principles from fluid mechanics to iterate on blade design and maximize efficiency.

Drone Building

My high school senior capstone project: I designed, fabricated, and successfully flew a custom 3D-printed drone entirely from scratch.

The frame was modeled in CAD and printed in PLA, with careful attention to weight distribution and motor mount geometry. I sourced and integrated the flight controller, ESCs, motors, and receiver, then tuned PID parameters for stable flight.

This was my first end-to-end hardware project and the experience that pushed me toward mechanical engineering.

ContextSenior capstone, Newtown High School
SkillsAirframe design, 3D printing, electronics integration, PID tuning
