About Me

Hi, I'm Jared.

I'm a sophomore at UConn studying Mechanical Engineering. Growing up, I spent a lot of time with my dad and grandfather, who always seemed to be in the middle of a project — either building something from scratch or figuring out how to fix anything that broke. That hands-on curiosity stuck with me, and it's why I'm pursuing a degree in mechanical engineering.

Over this past summer, I undertook a robotic arm project to explore my interest in robotics, gain experience in electronics and programming, and develop skills such as CAD modeling, 3D printing, and GD&T.

While working on this project, I worked as a Park Ranger with the CT Department of Energy & Environmental Protection. That experience was great for improving my people skills, learning how to manage public interactions, and contributing to the maintenance of the park's grounds.

I'm eager to combine these diverse experiences to make a meaningful impact as a Mechanical Engineer.
